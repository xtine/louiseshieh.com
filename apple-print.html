Apple print

Apple asked us to showcase the power and display of iPad and iPad mini in an engaging way. We came up with a campaign that featured just one of each product on each execution with two different apps that showed off the capability, potential, and stunning retina display of iPad. Had fun with the headlines to make sure that they applied to both iPad and the apps themselves.
