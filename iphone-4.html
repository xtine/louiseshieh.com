iPhone 4

An iPhone spot featuring a little bit of holiday magic with Santa on FaceTime.

In 2011, iPhone was available on Verizon as well as AT&T for the first time. Apple needed to announce this in a clear, non-competitive way and reassure people that each phone was as capable as the other. After only one short week, we concepted and produced this spot, celebrating the news. After all, two is better than one.
